We are a young family and new repatriates from Belarus. When we thought about which city to go to for life, we chose Haifa (of course, we understood that this might not be the last city in Israel to live in), this literally fascinated us and inspired us to create a new coffee shop in this beautiful city. It is with great pleasure that we want to share our interesting ideas with you and are always open to new ideas. The main concept of our institution is family, business meetings (working environment) and a place where you are always welcome! We are against racism and welcome everyone!
