Green tea-Classic green tea.

Strawberry tea -Strawberries are rich in ascorbic acid and vitamins from group B, it also increases immunity and has a light tonic effect and lifts the mood.

Karkade tea -Tea with an unusual taste and aroma, has unique properties, can be drunk hot and cold.

Sea buckthorn tea -One of the most useful berries containing a huge amount of beneficial substances. It is especially good to drink the infusion in the cold season, as it not only warms, but also increases immunity.

Cherry tea-From the first sip of this tea, you will feel a cherry explosion of taste and aroma.

Ginger lemongrass-Tea is ideal for summer, because it can be drunk cold. In cold tea, all the useful properties characteristic of a hot drink are preserved.

Milk green tea-Green tea with the aroma of milk will be a pleasant discovery for seekers of refined taste.

Jasmine tea-It has a sweet, but restrained and unobtrusive taste. Jasmine does not interrupt the natural smell of the tea leaf. They create a beautiful flavor composition in a pair, which you will definitely appreciate during a tea party.

White tea -It's a masterpiece. Tea that has been successfully grown, successfully prepared and successfully aged. He was lucky at all stages of his formation. This is a handsome man, which is not enough.

Chamomile tea-Tea with excellent taste and aroma, soothing and with a lot of medicinal properties.

Mulled wine-Warming soft drink with a whole hoard of spices, has a spicy taste and aroma.
